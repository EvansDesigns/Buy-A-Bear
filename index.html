Saving Bears, One at a Time


Buy-A-Bear is all about saving wild bears from the harsh consequences of humanity, whether it's a forest fire caused by a lit cigarette, forcing them to seek shelter in garbage dumps, or being hunted for sport, and brutally recycled into teddy bears for children. Buy-A-Bear provides an opportunity for people globally to purchase one of these bears and provide them with all of the "Bear" necessities that a bear could ever need.

It's simple. We provide the bears, you provide the food supply and shelter. (+ transportation fees)
